## Learned

- **Twilio trial accounts require a Verified Caller ID.** Ireland is
  fully blocked for verification via both SMS and voice call, per
  ComReg-adjacent restrictions on Twilio's side. Only unblock is to
  upgrade the account.
- **Twilio `<Say>` voice/language matrix is narrow.** `en-IE` is not
  supported. Amazon Polly voices only work under the language codes
  Twilio explicitly whitelists (`en-au`, `en-gb`, `en-in`, `en-us`,
  `en-za`, `en-gb-wls`). Fell back to `Polly.Amy`
